Noun clause
  • It...句子 + Wh句子，將Wh放前面（記得wh後面加名詞！） ，It放後面

    • 　What did you do in the garden last night? It is important.

    →What you did in the garden last night is important.

    　What are you eating? It looks delicious.

    →What you are eating looks delicious.

    　How are you going to get it back? It is my concern.

    →How you are going to get it back is my concern.

  • 非It開頭句子 + Wh句子，將非It放前面（ ，Wh放後面記得wh後面加名詞！）

    • 　Can you ask that man? Where is the post office?

    →Can you ask that man where the post office is?

    　Who gave him the book? She wondered.

    →She wondered who gave him the book?（由於who gave him the book有兼語的成分，故不需要調換位置）

    　Why is she angry with me? I cannot understand.

    →I cannot understand why she is angry with me.

  • If 和 Whether類型，要根據語境判斷，If不能放開頭
  • 剩下的就加that，不過還是要自己判斷哪句放第一，哪句放第二

    • 　Martin stole my watch. No one noticed.

    →No one noticed that Martin stole my watch.
